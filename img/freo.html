City of Fremantle Tourism Identity

PROJECT INFO

This was a project to devise an identity and branding for the tourism identity of the city of Fremantle, Western Australia. The city is known for its' diverse culture and left-leaning carefree inhabitants, as well its' beautiful scenery and deep beaches along the great coast. Another recognisable feature is the W.A. Maritime Museum which is also located within the city compass, and which I used the geometric shapes found in its' architecture for the development of a logo. I wanted to develop a fun and colourful logo identity to attract the young adults demographic, so I used basic shapes and flat colours with a slightly sandy texture to hint at the pervading beach culture which encompasses the area.

To go along with the logo, I designed layouts for the stationery and online portfolio for the tourism identity, staying with the simple shapes and grids and welcoming but lively colour schemes. The website and mobile app will allow potential tourists to peruse and book hotels located within the area, discover some information about the different tourist attractions, check out the upcoming local weather and even book at a number of restaurants located there. I wanted to create something simple and beautiful for such an appealing, yet often overlooked, destination for travellers.
