Candid E-Pub Magazine

PROJECT INFO

With the slow decline of print media, the e-pub format has become a fundamental component in the future of the written environment. Candid is an e-publication dedicated to the the arts and culture and devised specifically for the iPad. It contains journalism, objective interviews and news about all kinds of painters, writers, photographers and other multimedia artists. It is aimed at a more intellective crowd and each issue contains a showcase of a different artist work. The article I worked on contained a brief biography and opinion piece by a photographer about where art comes from both from a physical and an emotional perspective.

There are a number of challenges present when creating an e-publication which include a consistent magazine-like layout, creating both a vertical and horizontal reading view, and also what kinds of suitable 'effects' to publish for the digital format which might not distract from the writing. I added slide in/out title effects for the cover, slideshow 'narrative' effects for the storytelling of the article itself, scrolling text for the second half of the piece and a slideshow for the photography displays in between written pages. The article is available for viewing on request. It was created using Photoshop, Illustrator and Indesign.
