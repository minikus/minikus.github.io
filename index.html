Hello. My name is Carmen Minikus and I am a full stack web developer with a background as an industrial, UX, and graphic designer. I discovered my passion for coding whilst tinkering with Arduino and creating interactive products during my Industrial Design degree at (UTS).

In my final year of Uni, I took GA’s night time FEWD course, and shortly after I started a freelance agency with my partner called Serious Bear Designs. Whilst creating UX, branding, graphic and web design for SBD, I became hooked – the challenging yet creative possibilities of web design became my true passion and calling.

I'm a music nerd and love playing music to my dog, Wilco. I also love minimal designs, beautiful layouts, and illustration.
