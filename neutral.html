Neutral Awareness Campaign

PROJECT INFO

Neutral was an awareness campaign about the dangers of carbon emissions to the natural environment and how concentrated the expulsion of these emissions are from mass-populated urban areas of our planet. The elements of the promoted website include a way for visitors to calculate their own carbon footprint and then tips on how their own emissions can be reduced. The idea behind Neutral is that the world is in a sense falling out of balance and change needs to happen for it to be properly aligned before greater damage occurs.

As such, I wanted to create a branding that was at the same time hip to city culture but also quite natural and evironmentally friendly in terms of feeling and colour. I used a light wooden texture for the backgrounds and a few different green colours as well as a tidy grid structure which I think attracts the eye because of its simple elegance. I wanted to leave the design quite minimal and use a lot of white space because of the ideas of freedom, space and nature which I think are associated with the brand itself.
