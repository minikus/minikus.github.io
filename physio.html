Sydney Health Physiotherapy: Clinic Website

PROJECT INFO

Sydney Health Physiotherapy was the first website I ever made. It was created whilst I was studying industrial design at UTS, and undertaking General Assembly's Front End Web development course -and was created as my final project after the 10 week part-time course. The website was designed for a close friend who was about to open a physiotherapy clinic in Sydney's CBD.>

The website itself is a fully responsive portfolio website. The opportunity to create a website for an existing company was a big step in web design for me. The challenges, but also immediate gratifications of creating and seeing this live website within 2 weeks was one of the main reason that I decided to transition into web designer.
Check it out at: http://sydneyhealthphysiothearpy.com
